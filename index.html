About Us

Fruits come from the flowering part of a plant and contain seeds. In contrast, vegetables are the edible parts of a plant, such as the leaves, stem, roots, and bulbs. People often associate fruits with sweetness and vegetables with a savory taste.

Although this is often true, botanists classify some savory produce as fruits, such as tomatoes.
